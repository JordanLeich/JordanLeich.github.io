Escape within the lands of the great Anchorage

Tour Guide

Your Bears, Trains and Icebergs adventures start with a scenic ride on the Alaska Railroad's Glacier Discovery train. The train departs from the Anchorage Depot located at 411 West First Avenue at 9:45 am. After traveling through Anchorage, you will descend through the Anchorage Coastal Wildlife Refuge.

What To Wear?

Your trip includes professional guides, raingear, rubber boots, and a small snack. Dress warmly in layers as conditions around the icebergs can be chilly. Please bring thick socks (to wear with provided rubber boots), warm clothes (wear synthetic layers and bring a raincoat), a warm hat, and gloves. You may wish to bring binoculars and a camera if you have them.
