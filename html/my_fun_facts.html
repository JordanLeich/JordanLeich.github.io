Some Fun Facts About Me

Favorite Book: "The Outsiders" by Susan Eloise Hinton


Description: "The Outsiders" is a classic novel that delves into the lives of two rival teenage gangs in Oklahoma, exploring themes of friendship, family, and class conflict. I'm drawn to the raw and honest portrayal of the characters and their struggles to belong in a society that often labels them as outsiders.

Favorite Audio Track: "Sedona" by JunkBunny


Artist: JunkBunny
Description: "Sedona" is a song created by a very little known band of three young teenager boys called JunkBunny.

Favorite Movie: "The Dark Knight"


Description: "The Dark Knight" is a gripping superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. I'm captivated by the intense performance of Heath Ledger as the Joker and the moral dilemmas faced by Batman. It's a brilliant exploration of the blurred line between hero and villain.
